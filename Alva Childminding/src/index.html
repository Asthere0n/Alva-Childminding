What do we do?

Creative Arts and Crafts

Children love expressing themselves through arts and crafts. At Alva Childminding, kids can engage in activities like painting, drawing, and making collages. These activities not only foster creativity but also help develop fine motor skills. Using safe, non-toxic materials, children can create their masterpieces, which they proudly take home to show their families. The joy of creating something unique boosts their confidence and encourages imaginative thinking.

Outdoor Play & Exploration

Outdoor play is a vital part of the daily routine at Alva Childminding. Children can explore the well-equipped garden, participate in nature walks, and enjoy games like tag or hide-and-seek. These activities promote physical fitness, coordination, and social skills. The fresh air and natural surroundings provide a perfect backdrop for learning about the environment, observing plants and insects, and developing a love for nature.

Circle time & Group activities

Story time is a cherished activity where children gather to listen to engaging tales read by the caregivers. This activity not only enhances their listening skills but also sparks their imagination and love for reading. With a variety of books available, children can explore different genres and themes, from fairy tales to educational stories. Interactive reading sessions, where kids can ask questions and discuss the story, further enrich their learning experience.

Sensory Play

Sensory play activities are designed to stimulate children’s senses and support their cognitive development. At Alva Childminding, kids can engage in activities like playing with water, sand, and sensory bins filled with various textures. These activities help children explore and understand the world around them through touch, sight, and sound. Sensory play also encourages problem-solving skills and creativity, making it a fun and educational experience for the little ones.
